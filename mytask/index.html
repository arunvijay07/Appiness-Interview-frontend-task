Department of Cardiology at
Sakra World Hospital

TAt Sakra World Hospital, our team of the best cardiologists in Bangalore have dedicated their effort, time and expertise to people suffering with heart disease enabling them to lead productive and healthy lives.

We have an interventional team of the best cardiac doctors in Bangalore working round the clock providing timely and appropriate procedures such as primary coronary angioplasties and stenting. We also have an electrophysiology team with all matters relating to the field such as radiofrequency ablations, pacemakers and device implants, and resynchronisation therapy.

Why Choose Sakra?

The department of Cardiology at Sakra World Hospital, integrates compassionate care with state-of-the-art technologies. Our expert sub- specialty care is aided by the latest medical technology like three dimensional mapping and 128 slice Multi-detector CT which provides high resolution images of the coronary arteries enabling in early detection of heart disease. Cardiac MRI, 3D echocardiography and contrast echocardiography are some of the advanced diagnostic imaging technologies available here
